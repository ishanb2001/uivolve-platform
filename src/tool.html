How to Use the Email Builder

Welcome to the Email Builder! This tool helps you easily create and customize emails. You can add various sections, reorder them, and copy the layout to use in Outlook. Follow these instructions for a smooth experience:

1. Adding Sections to Your Email

To add sections to your email:

  • On the right-hand side, you'll see a list of available sections (like text, images, buttons, etc.).
  • Each option is labeled clearly (e.g., "Add Text," "Add Banner," "Add Footer") and will be added if you click on them.
  • Each added section will appear in the email preview and will also be listed in the right menu in the order they were added.

2. Rearranging Sections

You can reorder the sections in your email at any time:

  • In the right menu, you'll see arrows next to each section label (Up and Down arrows).
  • Click the up or down arrows to move the section up or down in the list.
  • The changes will be reflected in the email preview on the left, so the sections will be reordered accordingly.

3. Deleting Sections

If you want to remove a section from your email:

  • In the right menu, click the "Delete" button next to the section you want to remove.
  • The section will be deleted from both the email preview and the right menu list.

4. Copying the Email Format

Once you are happy with your email layout, you can copy the email to paste it into Outlook:

  • Click the Copy Format button to copy the email content and format
  • The email sections will be copied, maintaining the layout you’ve designed.

To paste the email into Outlook:

  • On a Mac, press Command + V to paste the copied content, then click the Paste Options icon (the clipboard) that appears and select Keep Source Formatting.
  • On Windows, press Ctrl + V to paste the copied content, then click the Paste Options icon (the clipboard) that appears and choose Keep Source Formatting.

5. Finalizing Your Email

After pasting the email into Outlook, you can continue to make the appropriate adjustments. You can further customize the email by editing the content directly in Outlook (adding content, hyperlinks,etc)
